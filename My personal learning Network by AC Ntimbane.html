Google Forms


Google Forms is a web-based application developed by Google that allows users to create, distribute, and analyze online surveys, questionnaires, and forms. It offers a user-friendly interface for designing custom forms with various question types, such as multiple-choice, short answer, and more. I used it to create short quizzes and tests for my students. It enables me to gather responses efficiently, making it easy to assess their understanding, tailor my teaching to their needs, and track their progress. Google Forms allows me to design customized forms with various question types, from multiple-choice and short answer to file uploads. I can also include images and videos, enhancing the engagement and clarity of the content. Furthermore, Google Forms is cost-effective as it is part of the Google Workspace suite, and it's easy to use, even for those with limited technical expertise. This accessibility ensures that both my students and fellow volunteers can interact with the forms without any difficulties.
